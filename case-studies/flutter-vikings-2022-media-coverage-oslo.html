About the event

Flutter Vikings 2022

Flutter Vikings 2022 was one of the largest Flutter and Dart community conferences in the Nordics — a two-day gathering of developers, tech leads, open-source contributors and experts from across Europe and beyond.

The conference ran three parallel tracks simultaneously, featuring keynote speakers, hands-on workshops, and community sessions. Attendance was both in-person at Felix Conference Center in Aker Brygge and remote, with sessions made available on YouTube for the global Flutter community.

Our role

End-to-End Media Coverage

Recap Media provided end-to-end media coverage for Flutter Vikings 2022 — from livestreaming all three tracks simultaneously to event photography across the full venue and session recordings for YouTube.

  • Multi-camera livestreaming of three simultaneous conference tracks with dedicated on-site capture
  • Event photography across the entire venue — speakers, audience, venue details, and community moments
  • Session recordings edited and delivered for publishing on YouTube
  • B-roll footage for social and promotional use
Results

Media That Keeps Delivering

The media package from Flutter Vikings 2022 has continued to deliver value long after the event:

  • Full session recordings published on YouTube for the global Flutter community
  • Social media content used by speakers, partners, and organisers across Instagram, Twitter/X, and LinkedIn
  • Website and community updates illustrated with event photography
  • Recordings continue to receive steady viewership — supporting future conference promotion
