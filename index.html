05 FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Our packages start at 15,900 NOK for a half-day shoot (Day Coverage), 57,450 NOK for the Conference Package, and from 89,000 NOK for Full Production. The final price depends on event duration, scope of coverage, deliverables, and turnaround requirements. We provide a detailed quote after a short briefing call — no surprises.
We handle photo, video, and livestreaming under one roof. Our Conference Package and Full Production packages include both photo and video as a combined crew — so you have one point of contact, one brief, and one consistent visual story across all deliverables.
We specialise in conferences, developer events, trade shows, corporate gatherings, product launches, and public/cultural events. We've covered everything from 20-person boardroom sessions to 500-attendee multi-track developer conferences. If it has a programme, speakers, or an audience — we can cover it.
Our primary service area is Greater Oslo — including Sandvika, Drammen, Lillestrøm, Jessheim, Holmestrand, and Moss. We also travel for larger conferences across Norway and internationally. Travel costs are agreed upfront and included in the quote.
Standard turnaround is 7 business days for edited photos and 14 business days for video. We also offer same-day social selects and a 24-hour fast-turnaround option for press-critical events — just let us know in the brief.
Max (the founder) attends every event and acts as the lead photographer/videographer and your single point of contact on the day. For larger events requiring additional crew, we work with a trusted roster of Oslo-based photographers and videographers who've been vetted through past projects.
