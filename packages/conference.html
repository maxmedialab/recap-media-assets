What's included

Conference Package

A two-person crew — one photographer, one videographer — covering your full conference day from registration to closing. You get a complete media package ready for press, social, and internal channels the same week.

  • Full-day on-site coverage (up to 8 hours)
  • 50+ edited photos — keynotes, panels, audience, networking
  • 2–3 min highlight film, professionally colour-graded
  • 5–10 vertical social clips (9:16, ready to post)
  • Digital gallery + video file delivery
  • 5–7 business day standard turnaround
  • Usage licence for digital, social, press, and internal use
