Full Production Package

End-to-end visual production from a dedicated crew. We handle the full content pipeline — capture, edit, and delivery — so your team can focus on running the event.

  • Full-day on-site (up to 10 hours)
  • Dedicated photographer + lead videographer
  • 80–120 edited photos — full event documentation
  • Up to 4 min cinematic recap film, fully colour-graded
  • 10–20 social media cuts (9:16 and 16:9 formats)
  • Optional: multi-camera livestreaming (2–3 cameras)
  • Optional: same-day selects or 24-hour full delivery
  • Digital gallery + all video files delivered
  • Usage licence for all channels — digital, broadcast, press, internal
  • Full pre-event brief, run-of-show review, on-site coordination

Livestreaming

Multi-Camera Live Coverage

Extend your event's reach beyond the room. Our livestreaming setup broadcasts to YouTube, Vimeo, LinkedIn Live, or a private stream — fully managed by our technical crew so your AV team doesn't have to.

  • 2–3 camera switched broadcast
  • Lower thirds and title cards (your branding)
  • Stream to one or multiple platforms simultaneously
  • Standalone VOD file delivered post-event
  • Technical run-through and venue connectivity check included

Livestreaming is an optional add-on to the Full Production Package. Pricing depends on venue setup, number of cameras, and platforms.
