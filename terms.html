3. Services

We provide event photography, videography, and livestreaming services as described in the agreed proposal. Services may include:

  • On-site photography and/or videography

We will make all reasonable efforts to deliver the agreed services. In the event of circumstances beyond our control (force majeure, illness, equipment failure), we will notify you promptly and propose solutions — including substitute crew or rescheduling.

4. Payment Terms

Deposit: A non-refundable deposit of 30% of the total project fee is required to confirm the booking and secure the date. The deposit is payable within 5 business days of accepting the proposal.

Balance: The remaining balance is invoiced upon delivery of the final deliverables and is due within 14 days of the invoice date.

Late payment: Overdue invoices may incur interest charges in accordance with the Norwegian Act on Interest on Overdue Payments (Forsinkelsesrenteloven). We reserve the right to withhold delivery of materials until full payment is received.

All prices are quoted in Norwegian Krone (NOK) and are exclusive of VAT unless otherwise stated. VAT will be added where applicable.

5. Cancellation Policy

Cancellations must be made in writing (email is sufficient). The following cancellation fees apply:

  • More than 30 days before the event: Deposit forfeited; no further charge

If we are required to cancel due to circumstances within our control, we will provide a full refund of all payments received, including the deposit. This is the limit of our liability in such cases.
